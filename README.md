## Description


Provide a short description explaining the what, why, and how of your project. Use the following questions as a guide:


- What was your motivation?
My motivation is that 1 in 3 US adults are obese. In inflationary times, you can't eat healthy on a budget. The only way to save money and eat healthy is to cook your own food.

- Why did you build this project?
I built this Meal Finder app to help individuals with recipes to cook in under 30 minutes.

- What problem does it solve?
The problem it solves is accessibility to a range of meals that are fast to make.

- What did you learn?
I learned how to connect two APIs and the use of Tailwind CSS.
